    A neat little RSS reader that sits on your Windows desktop

    Hello

    Feedling is a little Windows-based RSS reader that I wrote because I couldn't find anything that would do what I wanted it to do. Most RSS readers I had used were far too heavy and rich, whereas I needed something more light-weight. So, I wrote Feedling.

    It basically sits on your desktop, behind all your other windows displaying the latest headlines from the RSS feeds you configure. Glancing over the latest news is no more than a keyboard shortcut away. Clicking on headlines takes you to the relevent URL in your default browser.
